Impact

Overview

Supported by a Mozilla Responsible Computing Challenge grant, Responsible Datasets in Context has grown into a widely adopted resource used by instructors, students, librarians, and researchers across disciplines and institutions.

Awards & Recognition

  • Digital Project Award—American Studies Association — awarded to Responsible Datasets in Context for its contribution to digital scholarship in American Studies.

  • POEM (Project on Open and Evolving Metaliteracies) — forthcoming in this IMLS-funded open educational resource collection at Carnegie Mellon University.

  • Big Ten Data Visualization Championship (Love Data Week 2025) — our National Park visit dataset was selected as the featured dataset for this multi-institution data visualization competition.
